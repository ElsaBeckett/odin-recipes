Spaghetti Meat Sauce

Delicious Rich Spaghetti Sauce

This slow simmered spaghetti meat sauce will become your new go to for weeknight dinners. One pot and a simple prep means you can set it and forget it while cooking. It also makes a great freezer meal as the sauce can be frozen for up to 3 months and reheated whenever you need it.

Ingredients

  • 2 tblspn olive oil
  • 1 large carrot
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  • 1 stick of celary
  • 2 pressed garlic cloves
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatos
  • one can diced tomatos
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 2 cups of chicken or beef broth
  • 1/2 cup red wine
  • 1/2 tbspn soy sauce
  • salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Dice all veggies into small pieces
  2. In a large pot over medium heat pour in 1 tablespoon of olive oil and allow to warm
  3. Add diced veggies into pot and cook until softened and onions are translucent.
  4. Add garlic to the pot and cook until fragrant
  5. Deglaze pot with red wine and allow alcohol to cook off for about 5 minutes
  6. Now add both cans of tomatoes, dried herbs, bay leaves, salt & pepper, and soysauce to the pot. Give the sauce a good mix and then lower heat to a simmer
  7. Allow sauce to simmer for 40 minutes up to 3 hours. Add water if sauce gets too thick.
  8. Pour over pasta shape of your choice and enjoy!
